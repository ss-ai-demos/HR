Leave Process

  1. Check leave entitlement
    • Review the company’s leave policy (annual, sick, casual, etc.).
    • Confirm your available days and any blackout periods.
  2. Plan ahead
    • Choose dates that minimize disruption to your team or project.
    • Coordinate coverage if needed during your absence.
  3. Draft a leave request
    • Type of leave (annual, sick, personal).
    • Start and end dates.
    • Brief reason (e.g., family commitment, medical appointment).
    • Handover or delegation plan.
  4. Submit the request
    • Use the official channel (HR portal, email, or leave system).
    • Submit in advance unless it’s an emergency.
  5. Get approval
    • Wait for confirmation from your manager or HR.
    • Follow up if needed for time-sensitive requests.
  6. Prepare for absence
    • Complete pending tasks or delegate responsibilities.
    • Set up out-of-office replies (email and messaging).
    • Share emergency contact details if you’ll be reachable.
  7. Take leave
    • Disconnect as planned.
    • Respect boundaries unless you arranged to be partially available.
  8. Return and resume
    • Catch up on missed updates.
    • Thank colleagues who covered for you.
    • Submit any required documents (e.g., medical certificate).
